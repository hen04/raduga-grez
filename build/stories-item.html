How have you found this lockdown as a new mother? My son was born in January, so it feels like I have been in a bubble for a while now. As a new mother spending days centred around home, not too much has changed - however during level four my partner Morgan was home from work and we really treasured this time together. After having a baby I found that simple moments with family and friends was the best medicine, especially when feeling tired and adjusting to a new rhythm. Having their company or watching your child be loved and cared for by the people who are most important to you is a beautiful thing. Missing these moments has been the hardest part of lockdown.
